Holy Basil

Holy Basil, also known as Tulsi, is a herb widely used in Ayurvedic medicine. It promotes wellness and vitality. In the context of HoneyBurn, Holy Basil has been shown to significantly lower body weight, body fat, and blood sugar levels.

Olive Leaves

Olive Leaves contain a compound called oleuropein, which has anti-obesity effects. Oleuropein increases the production of adiponectin, a hormone that regulates glucose levels and fatty acid breakdown, thus aiding in weight loss.

Royal Jelly

Royal Jelly is a milky white substance secreted from the glands in the heads of worker bees. It suppresses appetite by increasing levels of satiety hormones such as leptin and cholecystokinin, promoting weight loss.

Sodium Alginate

Sodium Alginate is a natural fiber derived from seaweed. It reduces body weight and improves insulin sensitivity, reducing inflammation and oxidative stress in the body.

Carrot Powder

Carrot Powder, made from dehydrated carrots, is rich in essential nutrients, including vitamin A, vitamin C, antioxidants, and fiber. It combats obesity and reduces the amount of fat that accumulates in the body while increasing the body's ability to metabolize fat.

Propolis

Propolis is believed to reduce the amount of fat that accumulates in the body by preventing the activity of enzymes that play a role in the synthesis of fat. This results in lower fat stores in the body.
